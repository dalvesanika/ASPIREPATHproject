COURSE DETAILS: RELIGIOUS STUDIES

WHAT DOES RELIGIOUS STUDIES CAREER ENTAIL?

Religious Studies in the Indian context explores a rich tapestry of religious traditions including Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Buddhism, Jainism, Sikhism, and tribal beliefs. It focuses on the historical, cultural, philosophical, and ethical dimensions of religion in Indian society and beyond.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Comparative study of Indian religions
  • Scriptural analysis and interpretations
  • Religion and Indian socio-political life
  • Philosophy and ethics in Indian religions
Career Paths:

Career options include religious education, academic research, heritage and museum work, civil services, social work, journalism, and interfaith consultancy.
