COURSE DETAILS: MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

WHAT DOES MECHANICAL ENGINEERING CARRER ENTAIL?

Mechanical Engineering involves designing, analyzing, and manufacturing mechanical systems, from engines and machines to HVAC systems and robotics. It combines physics, materials science, and mathematics to solve real-world engineering challenges in industries like automotive, aerospace, energy, and manufacturing.

The program typically takes 5 years for a bachelor's degree, emphasizing hands-on training in workshops and labs. Graduates develop skills in CAD, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and machine design, making them versatile in various technical fields.
