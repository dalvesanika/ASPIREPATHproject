COURSE DETAILS: BUSINESS AND IT

WHAT DOES A BUSINESS AND IT CAREER ENTAIL?

A career in Business and IT in India integrates management principles with modern technology to drive digital transformation, business process optimization, and data-driven decision-making in organizations.

Professionals in this field act as a bridge between business operations and technological solutions, working on enterprise software, e-commerce systems, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and business analytics.

Students develop skills in IT project management, digital marketing, software development, database administration, and cybersecurity essentials.
