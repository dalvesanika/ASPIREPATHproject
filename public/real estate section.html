COURSE DETAILS: REAL ESTATE

WHAT DOES A REAL ESTATE CAREER ENTAIL?

A career in Real Estate provides students with knowledge in property management, real estate finance, investment, and valuation. Graduates can work as property managers, real estate agents, developers, or in real estate finance, helping clients buy, sell, or manage property.

Real Estate graduates learn about market trends, legal regulations, and property management, making them well-equipped to succeed in the dynamic real estate industry.
