COURSE DETAILS: MUSIC PRODUCTION

WHAT DOES MUSIC PRODUCTION CAREER ENTAIL?

Music Production is the process of creating, recording, and producing music. It combines technical and creative skills to craft music that meets professional standards. In India, this field is growing rapidly, with opportunities in film, advertising, digital platforms, and independent music scenes.

Specialized Areas:
  • Audio Engineering: Capturing, editing, and enhancing sound recordings.
  • Mixing & Mastering: Preparing audio for release by balancing and refining tracks.
  • Electronic Music Production: Producing music using DAWs like FL Studio, Ableton Live, etc.
  • Film Scoring & Sound Design: Composing music and sound effects for film and media.
