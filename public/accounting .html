COURSE DETAILS: ACCOUNTING

WHAT DOES AN ACCOUNTING CAREER ENTAIL?

An Accounting career in India involves financial reporting, auditing, taxation, and cost control. Students learn about Indian accounting standards, financial laws, and corporate governance.

Key Subjects:
  • Financial Accounting
  • Cost Accounting
  • Auditing
  • Taxation (Direct and Indirect)
  • Business Law
  • Accounting Information Systems
Career Paths:

Chartered Accountant, Auditor, Tax Consultant, Financial Analyst in both public and private sectors.
