COURSE DETAILS: BANKING AND FINANCE

WHAT DOES A BANKING AND FINANCE CAREER ENTAIL?

A career in Banking and Finance equips students with skills in managing financial institutions, investment analysis, risk management, and financial regulations. It prepares graduates to work in commercial banking, investment banking, financial planning, and corporate finance roles.

Banking and Finance graduates understand financial markets, corporate finance, and economic theory, making them valuable in managing financial systems both locally and internationally.
