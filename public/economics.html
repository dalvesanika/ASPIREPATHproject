COURSE DETAILS: ACCOUNTING

WHAT DOES AN ACCOUNTING CAREER ENTAIL?

An accounting degree prepares students for roles in managerial, financial accounting, taxation, and auditing. It includes key subjects like business mathematics, management theory, economics, and law. Graduates gain skills for various accounting positions in both the public and private sectors.

Accounting certifications like CPA, CFA, and CMA boost career prospects and earning potential.
