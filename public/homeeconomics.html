COURSE DETAILS: HOME ECONOMICS

WHAT DOES HOME ECONOMICS CAREER ENTAIL?

Home Economics in India encompasses the scientific management of home and community. It includes fields like food and nutrition, textiles and clothing, child development, resource management, and family relations. Professionals may work in education, health and nutrition sectors, NGOs, research, and media.

Specialized Areas:
  • Nutrition & Dietetics: Working in hospitals, health centers, and wellness clinics.
  • Textiles & Clothing: Careers in fashion design, merchandising, and research.
  • Child Development: Working in child care centers, NGOs, or early education institutions.
  • Family Resource Management: Involves budgeting, home planning, and consultancy.
