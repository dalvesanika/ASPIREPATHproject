COURSE DETAILS: LAND SURVEYING

WHAT IS LAND SURVEYING?

Land Surveying involves measuring and mapping land surfaces to establish boundaries, contours, and positions for construction, legal, and planning purposes. It combines fieldwork with advanced technologies like GPS, drones, and GIS software.

Key Skills:
  • Boundary determination and cadastral surveys
  • Topographic and engineering surveys
  • GNSS/GPS surveying techniques
  • Survey data processing and CAD drafting
Career Paths:

Licensed surveyor, land consultant, cartographer, or survey technician in construction firms, government agencies, or mining companies.
