COURSE DETAILS: LAND SURVEYING

WHAT DOES A LAND SURVEYING CAREER ENTAIL?

Land Surveying in India involves the measurement and mapping of land parcels to determine property boundaries, assist in construction, and facilitate land registration. It integrates traditional methods with modern technology such as GPS, Total Stations, GIS, and drones.

Key Skills:
  • Boundary and cadastral surveying
  • Topographic and engineering surveys
  • GIS and remote sensing applications
  • Survey data processing and AutoCAD drafting
Career Paths:

Career options include licensed land surveyor, GIS analyst, cartographer, government land records officer, and consultant in real estate, mining, or infrastructure projects.
