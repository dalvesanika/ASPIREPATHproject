COURSE DETAILS: BUSINESS AND MANAGEMENT

WHAT DOES A BUSINESS AND MANAGEMENT CAREER ENTAIL?

Business and Management focuses on the administration of organizations, aiming to enhance leadership, marketing, operations, and human resources. The field prepares individuals to manage teams, supervise business functions, and drive organizational growth.

The program covers key topics such as accounting, finance, economics, and strategic management, enabling graduates to pursue careers in diverse sectors such as banking, consulting, entrepreneurship, and corporate management.
