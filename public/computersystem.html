COURSE DETAILS: COMPUTER SYSTEMS ENGINEERING

WHAT DOES A COMPUTER SYSTEMS ENGINEERING CAREER ENTAIL?

Computer Systems Engineering integrates both hardware and software engineering. It involves the design, development, and maintenance of computer systems, including embedded systems, processors, network systems, and hardware/software integration.

Students develop expertise in system architecture, digital design, programming, and networks. This field prepares graduates for roles in industries such as embedded systems, telecom, IoT, and systems integration.
