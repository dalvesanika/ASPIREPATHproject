COURSE DETAILS: VIDEO GRAPHICS

WHAT DOES VIDEO GRAPHICS CAREER ENTAIL?

Video Graphics involves creating and editing visual content for media production. This includes animations, motion graphics, 3D models, special effects, and more. It plays a key role in films, advertisements, games, and digital platforms, blending creativity with technical precision.

Key Skills:
  • Animation and motion graphics
  • 3D modeling and visual effects
  • Video editing software (Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro)
  • Sound design and visual synchronization
Career Paths:

Motion graphics artist, video editor, VFX specialist, animation designer, or multimedia producer in Indian film studios, advertising agencies, gaming companies, and OTT platforms.
