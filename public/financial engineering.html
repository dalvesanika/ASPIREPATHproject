COURSE DETAILS: FINANCIAL ENGINEERING

WHAT DOES A FINANCIAL ENGINEERING CAREER ENTAIL?

Financial Engineering combines finance, mathematics, statistics, and computer science to build quantitative models and tools for risk management, investment strategies, and financial product design.

In India, this field is gaining traction in investment banking, fintech, and analytics-driven companies, where professionals develop algorithms for trading, portfolio optimization, and managing financial risk.
