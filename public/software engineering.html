COURSE DETAILS: SOFTWARE ENGINEERING

WHAT DOES A SOFTWARE ENGINEERING CAREER ENTAIL?

Software Engineering is the application of engineering principles to the design, development, and maintenance of software systems. It covers areas like programming, algorithms, systems architecture, and software testing. Software engineers work on a wide range of applications, from mobile apps to enterprise software and cloud-based systems.

Graduates of this field gain skills in coding, problem-solving, system design, and software development methodologies. Software engineers are in demand across various industries, including tech, finance, healthcare, and entertainment.
