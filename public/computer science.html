COURSE DETAILS: COMPUTER SCIENCE

WHAT DOES A COMPUTER SCIENCE CAREER ENTAIL?

A Computer Science degree provides students with the knowledge and skills needed to design, develop, and optimize software, systems, and technologies. Graduates can work in software engineering, systems analysis, cybersecurity, data science, artificial intelligence, and more.

Students learn programming, algorithms, computer systems, and mathematical foundations of computing, preparing them for roles in various technology sectors, from software development to IT consulting.
