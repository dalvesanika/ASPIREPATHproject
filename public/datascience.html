COURSE DETAILS: DATA SCIENCE

WHAT DOES A DATA SCIENCE CAREER ENTAIL?

A Data Science degree equips students with the knowledge to collect, analyze, and interpret large datasets to make informed decisions. Graduates can work in data analysis, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data engineering, among other areas.

Students learn programming, statistics, data modeling, and machine learning algorithms to solve real-world problems across industries like healthcare, finance, marketing, and technology.
