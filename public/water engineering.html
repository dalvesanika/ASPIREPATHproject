COURSE DETAILS: WATER ENGINEERING

WHAT DOES WATER ENGINEERING CAREER ENTAIL?

Water Engineering deals with the sustainable management of water resources including drinking water supply, wastewater treatment, irrigation systems, and flood control. It blends civil engineering with hydrology, fluid mechanics, and environmental science to address India's growing water challenges.

The 4-year B.Tech program in Water Resources Engineering includes training in hydraulic modeling, water infrastructure design, groundwater studies, and climate adaptation techniques. Graduates often find employment in government departments, NGOs, and private consultancies focused on water and sanitation solutions.
