COURSE DETAILS: BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

WHAT DOES A BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION CAREER ENTAIL?

A Business Administration degree in India prepares students for roles in management, entrepreneurship, and leadership. It focuses on key areas like marketing, finance, operations, human resource management, and strategic decision-making tailored to the Indian business environment.
