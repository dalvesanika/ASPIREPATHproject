COURSE DETAILS: SPACE EXPLORATION

WHAT DOES SPACE EXPLORATION CARRER ENTAIL?

Space exploration involves the investigation of celestial bodies beyond Earth using spacecraft, satellites, and space missions. It covers both human and robotic exploration of the solar system, the study of planets, moons, asteroids, and the possibility of life beyond Earth. The field plays a vital role in advancing science, technology, and our understanding of the universe.

Key Skills:
  • Aerospace engineering
  • Spacecraft design and navigation
  • Astrophysics and planetary science
  • Data analysis and mission planning
Career Paths:

Aerospace engineer, mission control specialist, space scientist, astronaut, or satellite technician, working for space agencies like ISRO, NASA, private companies, or research institutions.
