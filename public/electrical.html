COURSE DETAILS: ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING

WHAT DOES ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING CAREER ENTAIL?

Electrical Engineering is a core engineering discipline that deals with the study, design, and application of electrical systems, including power generation, electronics, signal processing, and telecommunications. It integrates physics, mathematics, and computer applications to build innovations in power and automation.

In India, the 4-year B.Tech program in Electrical Engineering includes hands-on training in circuit design, control systems, and power electronics. Graduates contribute to sectors like energy, manufacturing, electric vehicles, smart grids, and renewable energy.
