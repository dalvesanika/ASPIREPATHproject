COURSE DETAILS: HISTORY

WHAT DOES HISTORY CAREER ENTAIL?

History is the study of past events, particularly in human affairs. It explores the rise and fall of civilizations, the impact of key figures and movements, and the evolution of societies across time. Historians use written records, oral traditions, and archaeology to understand historical contexts and their relevance today.

Key Skills:
  • Research and critical thinking
  • Historical analysis
  • Understanding primary and secondary sources
  • Writing and communication skills
Career Paths:

Historians can work as museum curators, archivists, educators, researchers, or in roles within historical societies, governments, or media outlets.
