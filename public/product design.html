COURSE DETAILS: PRODUCT DESIGN

WHAT DOES PRODUCT DESIGN CAREER ENTAIL?

Product Design involves the process of imagining, creating, and iterating products that solve users’ problems or address specific needs in a given market. It blends creativity, technical skills, and user research to develop innovative products.

Specialized Areas:
  • Industrial Design: Designing physical products for mass production.
  • User Experience (UX) Design: Creating products that offer meaningful user experiences.
  • 3D Modeling & Prototyping: Turning ideas into tangible forms using digital and physical models.
