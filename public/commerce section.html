COURSE DETAILS: COMMERCE

WHAT DOES A COMMERCE CAREER ENTAIL?

A degree in Commerce prepares students for careers in business management, economics, finance, and marketing. It includes core subjects like accounting, business law, marketing, and organizational behavior. Graduates acquire skills in managing business operations, analyzing market trends, and making strategic decisions.

Commerce graduates can work in various industries, including finance, marketing, human resources, and entrepreneurship. They can also pursue professional certifications like CA (Chartered Accountant), CMA (Cost Management Accountant), and CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) to enhance their career prospects.
