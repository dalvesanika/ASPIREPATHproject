Course Details: Civil Engineering

What Does a Civil Engineering Career Entail?

Civil Engineering involves planning, designing, constructing, and maintaining physical infrastructure such as roads, bridges, dams, and buildings. It integrates scientific knowledge with real-world applications to support urban development, sustainability, and national growth.

The 4-year B.Tech program in Civil Engineering focuses on structural analysis, environmental engineering, transportation systems, and geotechnical studies. Graduates can pursue careers in government agencies, construction companies, and infrastructure consultancy firms.
