COURSE DETAILS: INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

WHAT DOES AN IT CAREER ENTAIL?

An IT degree prepares students to work in the design, implementation, and management of computer systems, software, and networks. IT professionals work in areas such as network administration, cybersecurity, database management, and system architecture.

Students learn about computer systems, programming, data management, networking, and information security to meet the growing technological demands of businesses and organizations.
